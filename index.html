TRAFFIC DRAMA PRESENTATION

At an unknown location within Uganda, Officer A stopped a speeding lorry. The driver stepped down already angry like he had argued with traffic lights before arriving. One word led to another — and suddenly Officer A and the driver were rolling in the dust like they were rehearsing for a wrestling championship. Two officers rushed in to separate them… but instead of separating them, they somehow joined the spinning confusion. From a distance, it looked like a dance rehearsal nobody had planned. After some dramatic pushing and heavy breathing, they dusted themselves and called it “a practical road safety lesson.”

This story comes to you all away from Kisozi Farm.
